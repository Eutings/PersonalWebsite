The most liveable area in London

This geographical data analysis project used GIS software to find that some areas in Lewisham meet low crime rates and are close to subways (800m), hospitals (2km), and parks (500m). Considered to be a relative location within London

Step 1:Create transport Buffer. describe: The blue background represents the map of London. Black lines are used to delineate different areas, and the purple overlays indicate the London transportation routes with an 800-meter buffer.

Step 2:Create hospital Buffer. describe: red circles mark a 2-kilometer buffer centered on hospitals.

Step 3: Create Garden Buffer. describe: brown circles represent a 500-meter buffer zone centered around park entrances.

Step 4: Intersection of buffer zones. describe: the green areas indicate the overlap of the aforementioned three buffer zones.

Step 5: Remove locations with excessively high or low population density, and identify areas with low crime rates. describe: the deeper the red, the higher the number of crimes. Black areas represent zones where the number of crimes in June 2023 was above 52 but below 958.

Result: The generated map shows that the intersection points within the buffer zones create an area with the desired characteristics in the red zones. Considering that the red area in Lewisham is relatively large, concentrated, and closer to the city center, Lewisham is considered an area with higher livability.
